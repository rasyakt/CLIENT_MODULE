How to Play

1

Players take turns placing hexagons with random numbers (1-20) on the board.

2

Red player goes first, then blue player alternates.

3

Capture: Place a hexagon adjacent to an opponent's hexagon with a higher value to take it over!

4

Boost: Place adjacent to your own color to increase its value by 1.

5

Score is the sum of all your hexagon values. Highest score wins!
